    Pokémon Ultimate Journeys: The Series to be available on Netflix from October 21st, Pokémon: The Arceus Chronicles now streaming

    The Pokémon Company International has announced that Pokémon Ultimate Journeys: The Series, the 25th season of the long-running Pokémon animated series, will premiere October 21st, exclusively on Netflix in the U.S. This season will continue to follow Ash Ketchum and his Pikachu, as well as Goh and Chloe, as they embark on their latest adventures in the Pokémon world.

    Trainers worldwide can encounter Spritzee in Pokémon GO by completing limited Timed Research marking the start of the Safari Zone: Goyang event

    To celebrate the Pokémon GO Safari Zone: Goyang event in South Korea, all Trainers worldwide are receiving limited Timed Research tasks from September 24th at 8am local time until September 25th at 8pm local time. By completing these tasks, Trainers will be able to encounter the Perfume Pokémon Spritzee. Particularly lucky Trainers may encounter its Shiny variant, which is making its debut with this event.
